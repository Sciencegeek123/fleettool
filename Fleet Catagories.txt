These are the fleet catagories I currently have: (They can easily be expanded as it's just entries in a database.)

* Strat Op: A fleet of the highest priority ordered by senior leadership.
* Offensive Fleet: A fleet with a targetted mission of agression against another entity.
* Defense Fleet: A fleet to defend MOON or allied assets, space, or members.
* Response Fleet: A fleet formed in response to an act of agression by another entity.
* Roaming Fleet: A fleet formed to seek out and engage other entities.
* Standing Fleet: A fleet formed for standard in pocket operations.
* PVE Fleet: A fleet formed to facilitate PVE.
* Indy Fleet: A fleet formed to facilitate industry or mining.
* Logistics Fleet: A fleet formed to facilitate logistics.
* Other: A fleet formed for some other reason. Please specify clearly in the description.
